AgroSafe – Smart Animal Deterrent System

A futuristic 3D printed stationary robot designed to protect agricultural lands and gardens from animal disturbances using smart deterrent technologies.


About AgroSafe

AgroSafe is an innovative 3D printed robotic system that remains fixed in one location and protects crops from animals, especially peacocks and other field intruders. It integrates multiple safe deterrent technologies such as light signals, sound emitters, sprinkler activation, and reflective mechanisms to prevent crop damage in an eco-friendly way.
